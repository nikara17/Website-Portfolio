I’m Nikara Ishwarlall, a BSc Information Technology graduate specialising in Robotics. My passion for technology is rooted in curiosity — understanding how systems learn, how data shapes decisions, and how engineering and creativity come together to create meaningful impact.

My academic foundation spans robotics, artificial intelligence, software engineering, and data analysis, giving me both analytical depth and structured problem-solving ability.
